# Mission

## Company mission

AIEOS will build and validate reliable autonomous AI Employees that own well-defined business workflows and deliver measurable outcomes under explicit human-governed constraints.

We will advance this mission by operating the first AI Employee ourselves, learning from real failures and results, and turning proven patterns into a reusable platform. AI will perform probabilistic reasoning and content generation; deterministic software will enforce permissions, workflow state, validations, safety rules, and business constraints.

## Initial mission: the YouTube Employee

The immediate mission is to build an autonomous AI YouTube Employee that can operate one fully AI-generated YouTube news channel for our internal team.

The intended user outcome is a consistently operated channel that publishes timely, factual, rights-respecting content without requiring a person to coordinate every production step. The initial operating target is:

- three Shorts per day; and
- one long-form video every alternate day.

Publishing volume alone is not success. The YouTube Employee must support content quality, factual verification, copyright controls, observable decisions, and recovery from failed or unsafe workflow steps. Human intervention should be explicit, purposeful, and measurable while the system earns greater autonomy.

## Current commitments

To move toward the vision, we will:

1. define an end-to-end operating workflow for the internal news channel;
2. automate it incrementally with clear permissions and validation gates;
3. measure reliability, content quality, distribution performance, intervention, and failure recovery during real operation;
4. improve the product using observed results rather than feature speculation; and
5. productize the YouTube Employee only after internal evidence supports a useful external offering.

## Mission boundaries

The mission does not require maximum autonomy at launch, support for every business function, or premature construction of a general-purpose platform. The current obligation is to prove that one AI Employee can deliver a valuable outcome responsibly and repeatedly, then reuse only what evidence shows is reusable.
